This classic spaghetti carbonara recipe brings the authentic flavors of Italy straight to your kitchen. Perfectly al dente spaghetti is tossed with a rich, creamy sauce made from eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and crispy pancetta. The subtle heat from freshly ground black pepper balances the dish, making it a comforting and indulgent meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 150g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (100g) grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced (optional)
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • Salt, for pasta water
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil the Pasta
  2. Prepare the Pancetta
  3. Mix the Egg and Cheese
  4. Combine Pasta and Pancetta
  5. Create the Sauce
  6. Season and Serve
